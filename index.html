Launch of Suraksha Kawach App

The Suraksha Kawach App was launched by Hon. Minister, WCD, Smt. Basanti Hembram on 26th Jan 2023.

State of the Art Technology

Safety Zone by Last Seen

The system keeps track of "last seen" status of each girl child. The data is collected through the app by Aanganbadi workers for age group (0 to 5) and (15 to 18). And for age group (6 to 14), the data is collected using school attendance. The system is smart enough to handle edge cases also. For example, if a girl child is in the group 6 to 14 but is on a vacation and is not attending school for a while, the system repoonds to it correctly.

Child Sex Ratio

Information related to birth and death is collected through the hospitals for male children also. This allows us to find out sex ratio and child sex ratio. The system reports the information descriptively for each area level. Like block wise, GP wise, Village wise and so on. It helps us find out the regions which are most affected and focus there.

School Attendance & Retention

Since attendance is taken for all the girl children in all the classes and schools everday, the system finds out the attendance rate, dropout rates and other useful information from it.
